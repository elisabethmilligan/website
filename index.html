Make Beeswax Wraps Yourself

  1. 1.) Ingredients: Beeswax Pellets, Cotton Fabric, Parchment Paper, Baking Sheet
  2. 2.) Cut the cotton fabric to your desired shape, for snack bags 7” by 14” is the best.
  3. 3.) Place cotton fabric on baking sheet and sprinkle beeswax pallets on the fabric
  4. 4.) Melt the pellets in the oven for 4-8 minutes on low heat. Then, brush the melted beeswax.
  5. 5.) Set the wrap to dry on a hanger for atleast an hour. Then, it’s ready to use!
